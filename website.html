ABOUT


Welcome to G's Musical Land! It is created for lovers of broadway musicals, especially those who are new to musicals! If you have no prior experience or if you have no idea what kinds of musicals fit your interest, take the matching quiz first! If you are curious about what musicals are currently showing or on national tour, the browse section will provide you everything you want to know! If you have already decided on what musical to watch, go straight to the ticket section and book your musical tickets in two steps! I hope you have a wonderful time at G's Musical Land!
